Fish at Depth (Web)

Simulates RGB attenuation with depth plus a simple pupil-compensation heuristic up to a luminance depth. Flashlight reveals the original image only within a circular region.
Notes:
  • Up to luminance depth, blue is held constant (compensation heuristic), red/green reduced “less” than raw attenuation.
  • Below luminance depth, raw attenuation applies beyond that point.
  • Flashlight uses drawImage + clipping (works correctly; putImageData does not respect clipping).
